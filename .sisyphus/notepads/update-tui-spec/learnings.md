## PromethION Layout Patterns
- 3000 channels arranged in 4 quadrants (2x2 grid).
- Quadrants: Q1 (63x12), Q2 (63x12), Q3 (63x13), Q4 (63x13).
- Dynamic cell sizing (1-char or 2-char) is a key requirement for responsiveness.
- Small terminal handling via partial display and '[...more]' indicator ensures graceful degradation.
